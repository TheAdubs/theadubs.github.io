About Me

I started my career on a Navy ship as an Information Systems Technician — responsible for the technology and, apparently, the morale of everyone around me. That combination of discipline, adaptability, and just enough irreverence has followed me everywhere since.

I've worked in television production (Warner Brothers/Telepictures), built marketing teams, closed deals, written strategies, and somewhere along the way became the person organizations call when they need someone who can think clearly and communicate even more clearly.

I'm a Catholic, a Krav Maga blue belt, and a deeply invested college basketball fan who applies more analytical rigor to March Madness brackets than most people apply to anything. My faith and my training both remind me of the same thing: stay grounded, stay ready, stay humble.

This site is a collection of things I've built, written, and thought about — equal parts professional portfolio and honest self-portrait.
