KMU Tracker

A full curriculum tracker for Krav Maga Worldwide belt progression — built for the practitioner who wants to train with precision, not just instinct. Rate your confidence on every technique, identify weak spots before testing day, and watch the curriculum unfold belt by belt.

Type Personal Training Tool
Built With React · localStorage · Vanilla CSS
Current Belt Blue Belt
Purpose Test Prep & Self-Assessment
What It Does
⚡
Confidence Rating System
Rate every technique 1–5 on a custom confidence scale — from "Just Heard of It" to "Muscle Memory." Progress saves automatically between sessions.
🎯
Weak Spot Analysis
Instantly surfaces your lowest-rated techniques sorted by belt level. Know exactly where to focus your next training session without guessing.
📋
Test Prep Mode
A focused view of your current belt's curriculum with technique notes, category breakdowns, and overall readiness scoring to prep for promotions.
📝
Technique Notes
Log instructor corrections, personal cues, and training reminders directly on any technique. No more forgetting what you learned last class.
🥋
Full KMW Curriculum
Every technique from Yellow through Blue belt, organized by category — strikes, defenses, ground fighting, self-defense, and tactical knowledge.
📱
Mobile-First Design
Built to be used on the gym floor between rounds. Readable on any screen, fast to navigate, and optimized for sweaty thumbs.
